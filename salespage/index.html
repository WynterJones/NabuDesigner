The four-stage method

Every page is designed the same disciplined way.

  1. 0

    Brief & Brand

    A single styleSignature sentence + a locked palette gets prepended to every prompt — so one coherent look, every asset, every time.

  2. 1

    Mockup & architecture

    Full persuasive copy and a layout archetype chosen to differ from its siblings. Every image is declared as a slot first.

  3. 2

    Asset generation & polish

    fal.ai makes premium stills, looping video, headline graphics and clean cut-outs — then each is post-processed locally and eyeballed.

  4. 3

    Motion, detail & QA

    Scroll reveals, parallax, particle fields, smooth scroll and micro-interactions — 60fps, checked at 360 / 768 / 1280, then shipped.
